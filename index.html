Capabilities

Spec Parsing

How It Works
Upload your bid package. Get answers.
Six-step analysis pipeline processes your specs and drawings together — just like your best estimator would, but in a fraction of the time.
01
Document Classification
Identifies specs, drawings, addenda, and supporting docs automatically.
02
Spec Parsing
Extracts requirements by CSI division — materials, submittals, testing, quality.
03
Drawing Analysis
Reads plan sheets and details to pull quantities, dimensions, and annotations.
04
Cross-Reference
Compares specs to drawings, finding conflicts, gaps, and ambiguities.
05
Estimate Framework
Builds line items with quantities, units, and cost data from public databases.
06
Checklist & Summary
Generates an executive summary, bid checklist, and exportable deliverables.
